I was once described as a nerdy, granola dude. This describes me pretty well. If you don't know what granola is, think of someone who likes to climb things for no reason and enjoys doing anything outdoorsy, like hiking, mountain biking or hammocking. They wear Birkenstocks or Tevas (oftentimes with socks) and shorts that are questionably short, but not too short. They go through phases of growing out their hair which I definitely have gone through. As far as the nerdy side of me, I am a Computer Science student with a Chinese minor. I enjoy building and fixing broken things as well as learning about different cultures and emersing myself in them. I like seeing the results of putting code together and what the product turns into using whatever creativity and resources I can muster. I spend my free time reading, running, and booling with my friends.
